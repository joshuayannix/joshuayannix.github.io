Crypto DCA Calculator

Full-stack MERN app

Allows user to see how much profit they would have made if they had made consistent dollar cost average investments in cryptocurrency. User can specify frequency, timeframe, and cryptocurrency. Taught me a lot about forms, number and date formatting, manipulating data from an API, interacting with a database. User can also download their results in a spreadsheet, as well as save/run their searches to/from a MongoDB database. Used Pusher for real-time updates from MongoDB, Redux for statement for the user.
