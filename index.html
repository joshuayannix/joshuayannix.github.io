Welcome! About Me

Self-taught programmer with extensive experience in highly-collaborative, fast-paced technology consulting and B2B tech sales

I've always been a self-starter, and I love learning new things, tinkering and figuring things out, and sharing my knowledge. Throughout college and my career, I've constantly had to teach myself new skills and reinvent myself. I have extensive experience working in highly-collaborative, fast-paced environments. I know how to manage multiple projects with tight dead-lines, how to manage up to my managers, and how to proactively take charge of my own learning and responsibilities so I can add value to the team as quickly as possible.

I used to think programming was only for Math whizzes. However, in my spare time, I started following free tutorials on the basics, and how to build simple web apps. It felt like a whole new world was opening up, and it was exhilerating learning new technologies and building my first simple To-Do lists. I am paritulcarly interested working in front-end development and React.

I am currently working as a Lead Workday HCM Consultant at TopBloc. Previously, I worked as a Business Development Representative, and then as an Account Executive, at Oracle-NetSuite. At Vassar College, I studied biology, chemistry, and economics, graduated with General and Departmental Honors, and was published in the Journal of Neuroscience for my Epilepsy research. Outside of work, I enjoy watching movies, spending time with friends, playing piano, saxophone and violin, and competing in amateur boxing.
